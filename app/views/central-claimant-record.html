Central claimant record

Design sprint Dec '21

Single claimant record design sprint December '21' miro board

This prototype looks at how we could group claimant information based on what an internal user needs to complete their everyday tasks. It provides a user with the following:

  • Click search on find claimant. No name or NINO required
  • Improved key detail bar to remove banner blindness and provide users with harmful information
  • Claimant overview
  • List of editable claimant data
  • Timeline
  • Claim history
